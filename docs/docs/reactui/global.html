PhoneButton(number, handleAvailable, showError)

Displays a button with a phone number and its linked bot. A free number will be in blue with green "Available" text, and an occupied number will be greyed out with grey "In Use: ..." text specifying the bot occupying that number. Occupied numbers have a tooltip to say they are occupied.
